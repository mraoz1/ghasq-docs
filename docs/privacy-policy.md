Ghasq Privacy Policy

Ghasq is a free Quran and prayer application.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting user information.

Information We Collect

Ghasq does not require account registration.

Ghasq does not collect personal information such as:

* Name
* Email address
* Phone number
* National ID
* Location data

Local Storage

Reading progress, Khatmah progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on the user’s device.

This information is not transmitted to our servers.

Notifications

Ghasq may send local device notifications for:

* Adhan reminders
* Prayer reminders
* Quran reading reminders

These notifications are generated on the device and are not used for advertising purposes.

Advertising

Ghasq does not display advertisements.

Third-Party Services

Ghasq may use trusted third-party services to provide Quran content, audio recitations, or related functionality.

Users are subject to the terms and policies of those providers where applicable.

Children’s Privacy

Ghasq is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the application developer.
